## Actionmap

**Actionmap** is a web application designed to empower citizens by providing easy access to information about their political representatives, upcoming political events, and news items relevant to their locality. Users can aggregate, share, and view news, fostering an informed and engaged community.

This application was built in Ruby over Rails using MVC and Agile methodologies as a final project for CS 169A Software Engineering at UC Berkeley with a group of five students.

### Features

- **Find Your Representatives**: Users can search for their local, state, and national representatives by entering their address. Detailed profiles for each representative include contact information, political party affiliation, and relevant news articles.

- **Local Political Events**: Stay up-to-date with political events in your area. The app aggregates event information so users can easily find town halls, rallies, and other political gatherings nearby.

- **News Aggregation**: View and share news articles relevant to your locality. The app curates news items from various sources, allowing users to select, rate, and comment on news articles.

- **User Profiles**: Create a personalized profile to save favorite articles, track engagement with representatives, and receive notifications about upcoming events.

### Installation

To run Actionmap locally, follow these steps:

1. **Clone the repository**
2. **Navigate to the project directory**
3. **Install dependencies**: Actionmap runs on deprecated versions of all languages and therefore might not work as intended when not running on the specified versions.
5. **Set up the database**
6. **Start the server**
7. **Visit the app**: Open your browser and go to `http://localhost:3000`.

### Usage

- **Find Representatives**: Enter your address on the homepage to find detailed information about your elected officials.
- **Explore Events**: Browse the events section to find and RSVP to local political events.
- **Aggregate News**: Search for news related to your locality, save your favorite articles, and share them with others.
- **User Profiles**: Sign up or log in to access personalized features, such as tracking your interactions with representatives.

### Testing

Actionmap includes both RSpec tests for the backend and Cucumber tests for feature testing.
