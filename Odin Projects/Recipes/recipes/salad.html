Salad

Description

This salad is a refreshing mix of greens, avocado, and a light vinaigrette. Perfect for a summer day!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups mixed greens
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese (optional)
  • 1/4 cup walnuts (optional)
  • Fresh herbs (basil, parsley, etc.) for garnish

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the mixed greens, avocado, cucumber, and red onion.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  4. If desired, sprinkle with feta cheese and walnuts.
  5. Garnish with fresh herbs before serving.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
