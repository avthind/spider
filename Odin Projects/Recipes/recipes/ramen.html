Ramen

Description

Ramen is a Japanese noodle soup dish that consists of Chinese-style wheat noodles served in a meat- or fish-based broth, often flavored with soy sauce or miso, and uses toppings such as sliced pork, nori (dried seaweed), menma, and scallions.

Ingredients

  • 2 packs of instant ramen noodles
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 2 soft-boiled eggs (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chili oil (optional)
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Steps

  1. In a pot, bring the broth to a boil.
  2. Add the ramen noodles and cook according to package instructions.
  3. Add soy sauce, sesame oil, mushrooms, and spinach. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve hot, topped with green onions, soft-boiled eggs, chili oil, and sesame seeds.
  6. Enjoy your homemade ramen!
