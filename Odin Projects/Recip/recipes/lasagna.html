Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and sauce. It's a hearty and comforting meal that's perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  5. Spread a layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, ground beef, mozzarella cheese, and marinara sauce. Repeat layers.
  7. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  8. Bake for 45 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown.
  9. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil.
